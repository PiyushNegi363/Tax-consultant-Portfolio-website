Before You Contact Us

Generally, you will need your PAN card, Aadhaar card, Form 16 (for salary income), bank account statements for the financial year, and investment proofs (80C/80D) for tax deductions. Depending on your specific income sources (like business, capital gains, or house property), we will provide a precise checklist.

GST registration typically takes 3 to 7 working days once submitted, subject to department processing times, portal verification, and prompt response to any clarification queries (queries from officers).

Most taxation consultations and filings can be completed securely via WhatsApp and email. An office visit is rarely necessary and is typically only required if physical verification of original statutory registry documents is requested by department authorities.
